* Utils

** Convert Chinese Quotations

#+BEGIN_SRC elisp
(defun xandeer/convert-chinese-quotations ()
  "Convert all [“|“] to [『|』] in current buffer."
  (interactive)

  (goto-char (point-min))
  (while (re-search-forward "“" nil t)
    (replace-match "「"))

  (goto-char (point-min))
  (while (re-search-forward "”" nil t)
    (replace-match "」")))
#+END_SRC
